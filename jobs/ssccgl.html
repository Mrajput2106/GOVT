SSC CGL Recruitment 2025

Post Date: July 4, 2025 | 10:45 AM

Overview

Staff Selection Commission (SSC) has released the notification for the Combined Graduate Level (CGL) Examination 2025. The recruitment is being conducted for 14,582 positions. Candidates can apply online from 09 June 2025 to 04 July 2025.

Important Dates

  • Start Date to Apply Online: 09 June 2025
  • Last Date to Apply Online: 04 July 2025
  • Last Date for Fee Payment: 05 July 2025
  • Correction Date: 09 - 10 July 2025
  • Exam Date: 13 - 30 August 2025
  • Admit Card: Before Exam
  • Result Date: Will be updated soon

Application Fee

  • General / OBC / EWS: ₹100/-
  • SC / ST / Female / PH: ₹0/-
  • Payment Mode: Debit Card, Credit Card, Net Banking, IMPS, Wallet

Age Limit (as on 01 August 2025)

  • Minimum Age: 18 Years
  • Maximum Age: 30 Years
  • Age relaxation available as per SSC rules

Total Posts

14,582 Posts

Vacancy & Eligibility Details

Post Name Eligibility Criteria
All Other Posts Graduation in any stream from a recognized university
Junior Statistical Officer Bachelor’s degree in any stream with at least 60% marks in Mathematics in 12th OR Bachelor's with Statistics as one subject

How to Apply

  • Apply online through the SSC official website before 04 July 2025
  • Or use the direct application link under the important links section

Documents Required

  • Passport-sized photo (light/white background)
  • Signature (black/blue pen on white paper)
  • 10+2 certificates and graduation documents
  • Caste/EWS certificate if applicable
  • ID Proof (Aadhaar/Voter ID)
  • Domicile certificate (for Rajasthan candidates)
  • Income certificate (for EWS)
  • Special category certificates (PH/Ex-servicemen)

Mode of Selection

  1. Tier-I Exam
  2. Tier-II Exam
