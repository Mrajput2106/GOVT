Bank Of Baroda BOB LBO Recruitment 2025

Post Date: July 4, 2025 | 1:09 PM

Short Overview

Bank of Baroda (BOB) has released a notification for the recruitment of Local Bank Officer (LBO) posts. A total of 2500 positions are available. The application process begins on 04 July 2025 and ends on 24 July 2025.

Important Dates

  • Online Apply Start Date: 04 July 2025
  • Last Date to Apply Online: 24 July 2025
  • Last Date for Fee Payment: 24 July 2025
  • Exam Date: Notify Soon
  • Admit Card: Before Exam
  • Result Date: Will Be Updated Soon

Application Fee

  • General/OBC/EWS: ₹ 850/-
  • SC/ST/PwBD: ₹ 175/-
  • Payment Mode: Debit Card, Credit Card, Net Banking, IMPS, Wallet

Age Limit (as on 01 July 2025)

  • Minimum Age: 21 Years
  • Maximum Age: 30 Years
  • Relaxation applicable as per rules

Total Posts

2500 Posts

Vacancy Details

Post Name No. of Posts
Local Bank Officer (LBO) 2500

Eligibility Criteria

  • Graduate or professional qualification (CA, Engineering, Medical)
  • Minimum 1 year experience as officer in Scheduled/Regional Rural Bank
  • Proficiency in local language is mandatory

How to Fill BOB LBO Online Form

  • Apply online via the official BOB website or click the direct link provided
  • Submit your application before 24 July 2025

Documents Required

  • Passport-size photo (white/light background)
  • Signature (black/blue pen on white paper)
  • 10th, 12th, Graduation/Degree certificates
  • Professional/PG certificates (if applicable)
  • Caste/EWS/PWD certificate (if applicable)
  • ID Proof (Aadhaar/Voter ID)
  • Domicile Certificate (for Rajasthan candidates)
  • Income Certificate (for EWS)
  • Date of Birth Proof
  • Valid Email ID and Mobile Number

Selection Process

  1. Online Examination
  2. Psychometric Test (Personality Assessment)
  3. Personal Interview
  4. Final Merit List

Note: Candidates must visit the BOB official website regularly for updates.
